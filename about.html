Our Story

Built on Innovation, Driven by Results

Founded in 2010, Stackly Business Solutions began with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between ambitious business goals and transformative technology. What started as a boutique consulting firm has evolved into a global enterprise partner trusted by Fortune 500 companies and fast-growing startups alike.

From our headquarters in Tech City, we've delivered over 500 successful digital transformations across 40+ countries. Our multidisciplinary teams combine deep industry expertise with cutting-edge engineering to solve complex challenges at scale.

  • ISO 27001 certified with enterprise-grade security practices
  • Strategic partnerships with AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud
  • Dedicated client success teams with 98% retention rate
  • Agile delivery methodology with transparent project governance
